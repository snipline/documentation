Billing

Trial

How long does the trial last?

The trial lasts 30 days unless otherwise mentioned.

Do I need to give my credit card details to sign up?

No, you can trial Snipline risk-free.

What happens when the trial is over?

If you have not purchased a subscription you will be asked to do so before continueing to use the app.

If you have purchased a subscription then service will continue as normal.

My trials ran out and I didn't get a chance to try it

Send us an email and we'll see what we can do.

Subscriptions

How do I subscribe?

Go to your account dashboard and from there, go to settings and then the subscription tab. Direct link here

When will I be billed?

Billing starts as soon as you purchase the subscription. Subscriptions are then handled by Stripe and will reoccur yearly.

How do I cancel?

Go to your account dashboard and from there, go to settings and then the subscription tab. Click the Cancel Subscription button. Direct link here

I want a refund

Sorry to hear that! Please contact us by email and we'll get that resolved for you.
